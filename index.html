About The Map And Its Creators

This project was a collaborative effort from a group of students at the University of Oregon. The project was put together to help support efforts by the environmental group, Beyond Toxics, to regulate aerial herbicide spraying in Oregon State Forests. Beyond Toxics is a non-profit group based out of Eugene Oregon, who spearheaded this legislative effort and aided in this project’s development. We conducted this project for credit within the Geospatial Project Design course in the Department of Geography at the University of Oregon, and were supervised by Professor Carolyn Fish. All spatial data was gathered from the Oregon Department of Forestry’s FERN database and the Oregon Spatial Data Library. Our group represents a diverse range of perspectives, with backgrounds in Geography, Spatial Data Science, Economics, Computer Science, Planning, Public Policy, and Management, and Environmental Science.

Creators: Arielle Alferez, Giancarlo DiTomasso, Madison Fung, Azoline Gaudin-Dalton, Hunter Mackin, Andrew Wise
